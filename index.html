Meredith Hopkins

Meredith is an aspiring data scientist currently studying data analysis and visualization in a University of Pennsylvania Bootcamp. Her previous education includes a BA in East Asian Studies through UMGC, an AA in Music Industry Studies through HACC, and a certificate in Audio Engineering through PARAS. She attended Middlebury Language School's summer Chinese program in 2021 and speaks limited Mandarin. Academic accomplishments of note include many semesters on the Dean's List at both HACC and UMGC, graduating summa cum laude from UMGC with a 4.0, and having the honor of being graduation speaker on behalf of the level one students at Middlebury's 2021 summer Chinese program.

Meredith's most recent employment was 5 years as a library assistant in a public library; she left that job to study full-time in pursuit of a new career in data science. In her role as a library assistant, her responsibilities included readers advisory, planning and hosting public programs, curating displays, doing monthly statistics for her department, and materials handling.
